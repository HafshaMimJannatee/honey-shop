Our Story

Family-Owned Since 1985

Golden Hive began as a small family operation with just a few beehives in our backyard. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a passion for sustainable beekeeping and producing the highest quality honey.

Today, we maintain over 200 hives across Northern California's most pristine wildflower meadows, always practicing ethical beekeeping methods that prioritize the health of our bees and the environment.

Our honey is raw, unfiltered, and never heated above hive temperature, preserving all the natural enzymes, antioxidants, and health benefits.
